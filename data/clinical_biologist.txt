Job Title: Clinical Biologist

About Us:
We are a leading company in biotechnology and clinical research looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to advance medical understanding and develop novel therapeutic solutions.

The Role:
As a Clinical Biologist, you will be responsible for designing and conducting biological experiments, analyzing biological samples and data, and contributing to clinical study design and execution. You will work closely with clinical research and biology teams to generate critical insights for drug discovery and patient care. This role requires a strong commitment to scientific accuracy and patient impact.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design and execute in vitro and in vivo biological experiments relevant to disease mechanisms.
- Perform molecular biology techniques (e.g., qPCR, Western blot, ELISA) and cell culture.
- Analyze complex biological data, interpret results, and prepare scientific reports and presentations.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Biology, Molecular Biology, Biochemistry, Pharmacology, or a related Life Sciences field or equivalent practical experience.
- 3+ years of proven professional experience in Clinical Biologist or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Cell Culture, Molecular Biology Techniques, Assay Development.
- Demonstrated experience with Data Analysis (e.g., GraphPad Prism, R), Experimental Design, Scientific Writing.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Flow Cytometry, Microscopy, CRISPR, Gene Editing, Clinical Trial experience.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- contributing to the development of life-changing therapies.
