Job Title: Software Engineer

Job Summary:
As a Software Engineer, you will be responsible for designing scalable software systems, developing high-quality code, and participating in code reviews. You will work closely with development team to deliver innovative software products.

- Develop and maintain robust software applications using Python and Java.
- Implement RESTful APIs and microservices architectures.
- Write unit and integration tests to ensure code quality.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams.
- Ensure high quality and timely delivery of projects.
- Stay updated with industry best practices and emerging technologies.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field.
- X+ years of proven experience in Software Engineer or a similar role.
- Strong proficiency in Python, Java, Spring Boot.
- Experience with REST APIs, Microservices, SQL databases.
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Docker, Kubernetes, AWS, Git, Agile Scrum.
- Certifications relevant to the role.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.
