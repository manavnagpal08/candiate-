Job Title: Senior Software Engineer

About Us:
We are a leading company in technology solutions looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to build innovative and reliable software products.

The Role:
As a Senior Software Engineer, you will be responsible for designing scalable software systems, developing high-quality, maintainable code, and participating in architectural discussions and code reviews. You will work closely with development team to deliver robust and performant software solutions. This role requires a strong commitment to scalability and code quality.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design, develop, and maintain backend services and APIs using Python, Java, or Go.
- Implement efficient algorithms and data structures for optimal performance.
- Write comprehensive unit, integration, and end-to-end tests.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering or equivalent practical experience.
- 3+ years of proven professional experience in Senior Software Engineer or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Python, Java, Go, or C++.
- Demonstrated experience with RESTful APIs, Microservices, SQL/NoSQL databases, Git.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Docker, Kubernetes, AWS/Azure/GCP, CI/CD pipelines, Agile Scrum.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- working with cutting-edge technologies and solving complex engineering challenges.
