Job Title: Supply Chain Manager

About Us:
We are a leading company in supply chain and logistics looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to optimize our global supply chain for efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and resilience.

The Role:
As a Supply Chain Manager, you will be responsible for overseeing end-to-end supply chain operations, managing procurement and vendor relationships, and optimizing inventory and logistics processes. You will work closely with operations and supply chain teams to enhance supply chain performance and reduce operational costs. This role requires a strong commitment to efficiency and strategic planning.

Key Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement supply chain strategies to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and mitigate risks.
- Manage procurement processes, including supplier selection, contract negotiation, and performance monitoring.
- Oversee logistics, warehousing, and transportation, ensuring timely and cost-effective delivery.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Supply Chain Management, Logistics, Business, or Engineering or equivalent practical experience.
- 5+ years of proven professional experience in Supply Chain Manager or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Supply Chain Optimization, Procurement, Inventory Management, Logistics.
- Demonstrated experience with ERP Systems (e.g., SAP, Oracle), Vendor Management, Data Analysis.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with APICS CSCP/CPIM, Demand Forecasting, Global Supply Chain experience, Lean/Six Sigma.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- driving strategic improvements across a complex global supply chain.
