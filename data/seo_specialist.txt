Job Title: SEO Specialist

About Us:
We are a leading company in digital marketing and search engine optimization looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to maximize organic search visibility and drive qualified traffic to our digital properties.

The Role:
As a SEO Specialist, you will be responsible for conducting comprehensive keyword research and analysis, implementing on-page and technical SEO strategies, and monitoring SEO performance and providing actionable insights. You will work closely with digital marketing team to improve search engine rankings and increase organic traffic. This role requires a strong commitment to analytical precision and continuous optimization.

Key Responsibilities:
- Perform in-depth keyword research, competitive analysis, and audience analysis to identify SEO opportunities.
- Implement on-page SEO best practices (content optimization, meta tags) and technical SEO (site architecture, crawlability).
- Monitor SEO performance using tools like Google Analytics, Search Console, and SEMrush, providing regular reports.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Marketing, Digital Marketing, or a related analytical field or equivalent practical experience.
- 2+ years of proven professional experience in SEO Specialist or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in SEO (On-page, Technical, Off-page), Keyword Research, Google Analytics.
- Demonstrated experience with Google Search Console, SEMrush/Ahrefs, Content Optimization, Link Building.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with HTML/CSS basics, WordPress/CMS experience, Content Marketing, A/B Testing.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- driving significant organic growth and expanding our digital footprint.
