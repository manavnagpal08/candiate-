Job Title: Game Developer

About Us:
We are a leading company in interactive entertainment and video game development looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to create immersive and engaging gaming experiences for players worldwide.

The Role:
As a Game Developer, you will be responsible for developing game mechanics and systems, writing clean and optimized game code, and collaborating with artists and designers to bring game concepts to life. You will work closely with game development team to deliver high-quality and fun games. This role requires a strong commitment to creativity and technical excellence.

Key Responsibilities:
- Implement core game mechanics, AI behaviors, and user interfaces using Unity or Unreal Engine.
- Write efficient and robust code in C# (for Unity) or C++ (for Unreal Engine).
- Collaborate with game designers, artists, and QA to iterate on features and resolve bugs.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Game Development, or a related field or equivalent practical experience.
- 2+ years of proven professional experience in Game Developer or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Unity/Unreal Engine, C#/C++, Game Mechanics Implementation.
- Demonstrated experience with Object-Oriented Programming, Data Structures, Version Control (Git).
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Shader Programming, Networking (multiplayer games), Physics Engines, Mobile Game Development.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- bringing creative visions to life and entertaining millions of players.
