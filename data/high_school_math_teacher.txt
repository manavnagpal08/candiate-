Job Title: High School Math Teacher

About Us:
We are a leading company in secondary education looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to inspire and empower students to achieve academic excellence and critical thinking skills.

The Role:
As a High School Math Teacher, you will be responsible for developing engaging math lesson plans and curriculum, delivering effective instruction to diverse student populations, and assessing student progress and providing constructive feedback. You will work closely with Mathematics department and school faculty to foster a deep understanding and appreciation for mathematics. This role requires a strong commitment to student engagement and academic rigor.

Key Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement comprehensive lesson plans for various high school math subjects (e.g., Algebra, Geometry, Calculus).
- Deliver engaging and differentiated instruction to meet the needs of all students.
- Assess student learning through various methods, provide timely feedback, and track academic progress.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Mathematics Education, Secondary Education with a Math focus, or Mathematics or equivalent practical experience.
- 1+ year of proven professional experience in High School Math Teacher or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Lesson Planning, Classroom Management, Differentiated Instruction.
- Demonstrated experience with Algebra, Geometry, Calculus, Statistics, Student Assessment.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Educational Technology (e.g., Desmos, GeoGebra), Curriculum Development, Parent-Teacher Communication.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- shaping young minds and fostering a passion for learning.
