Job Title: Digital Marketing Specialist

About Us:
We are a leading company in digital marketing and e-commerce looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to drive brand awareness, engagement, and customer acquisition.

The Role:
As a Digital Marketing Specialist, you will be responsible for developing and executing digital marketing campaigns, managing social media presence and content, and analyzing campaign performance and optimizing for ROI. You will work closely with marketing department to increase online visibility and lead generation. This role requires a strong commitment to creativity and data-driven optimization.

Key Responsibilities:
- Plan, execute, and optimize paid advertising campaigns (Google Ads, Facebook Ads).
- Manage SEO efforts, including keyword research, on-page optimization, and link building.
- Create engaging content for various digital channels and manage email marketing campaigns.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Marketing, Communications, or Business or equivalent practical experience.
- 2+ years of proven professional experience in Digital Marketing Specialist or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in SEO, SEM, Social Media Marketing, Email Marketing.
- Demonstrated experience with Google Ads, Facebook Ads, Google Analytics, Content Creation.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with HubSpot, Salesforce Marketing Cloud, A/B Testing, Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO).
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- contributing to direct business growth through innovative digital strategies.
