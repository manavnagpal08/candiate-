Job Title: UX/UI Designer

About Us:
We are a leading company in digital product design and user experience looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to create intuitive, engaging, and accessible digital products.

The Role:
As a UX/UI Designer, you will be responsible for conducting user research and usability testing, creating wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity UI designs, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to deliver exceptional user experiences. You will work closely with design, product, and engineering teams to enhance product usability and user satisfaction. This role requires a strong commitment to user-centricity and visual excellence.

Key Responsibilities:
- Plan and conduct user research (interviews, surveys, usability tests) to inform design decisions.
- Translate research insights into user flows, wireframes, interactive prototypes, and polished UI mockups.
- Contribute to and maintain design systems, ensuring consistency and efficiency across products.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Interaction Design, Graphic Design, Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) or equivalent practical experience.
- 3+ years of proven professional experience in UX/UI Designer or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in User Research, Wireframing, Prototyping, UI Design, UX Design.
- Demonstrated experience with Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD, Usability Testing, Responsive Design.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Design Systems, Accessibility Standards, Motion Graphics.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- designing innovative solutions that directly impact user lives.
