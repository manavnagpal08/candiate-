Job Title: UX Researcher

About Us:
We are a leading company in user experience and product innovation looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to deeply understand user needs and behaviors to inform product design.

The Role:
As a UX Researcher, you will be responsible for planning and conducting comprehensive user research studies, analyzing qualitative and quantitative data to generate insights, and communicating actionable research findings to product teams. You will work closely with UX Research and Product Design teams to drive user-centered product development and strategy. This role requires a strong commitment to user empathy and rigorous methodology.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design and execute various research methods, including usability testing, interviews, surveys, and field studies.
- Analyze qualitative and quantitative data to identify user behaviors, motivations, and pain points.
- Present compelling research findings and actionable recommendations to product, design, and engineering teams.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Psychology, Anthropology, Design, or a related field or equivalent practical experience.
- 3+ years of proven professional experience in UX Researcher or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in User Research Methodologies, Usability Testing, Interviewing, Survey Design.
- Demonstrated experience with Qualitative/Quantitative Data Analysis, Figma/Sketch/Adobe XD (for prototyping review), A/B Testing.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Lookback, UserTesting.com, Miro, Confluence, Statistical Analysis (R/Python).
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- being the voice of the user and shaping truly impactful products.
