Job Title: Human Resources Generalist

About Us:
We are a leading company in talent management and employee experience looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to foster a supportive, inclusive, and high-performing workplace culture.

The Role:
As a Human Resources Generalist, you will be responsible for managing employee relations and engagement initiatives, supporting the full talent acquisition lifecycle, and administering HR policies and benefits programs. You will work closely with HR department to ensure a positive and compliant employee experience. This role requires a strong commitment to empathy and compliance.

Key Responsibilities:
- Provide guidance and support to employees and managers on HR policies, procedures, and best practices.
- Assist with recruitment efforts, including sourcing, interviewing, onboarding, and offboarding processes.
- Manage HR administrative tasks, including record-keeping, payroll support, and benefits administration.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or Organizational Development or equivalent practical experience.
- 3+ years of proven professional experience in Human Resources Generalist or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Employee Relations, Talent Acquisition, HR Policies & Compliance (e.g., FMLA, ADA).
- Demonstrated experience with Performance Management, HRIS (Human Resources Information Systems), Benefits Administration.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Workday, ADP, BambooHR, Microsoft Office Suite, Conflict Resolution.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- shaping a thriving workplace culture and supporting employee growth.
