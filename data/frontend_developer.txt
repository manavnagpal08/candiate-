Job Title: Frontend Developer

About Us:
We are a leading company in web application development looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to build intuitive and high-performance user interfaces.

The Role:
As a Frontend Developer, you will be responsible for developing responsive web applications, implementing user-facing features, and optimizing web performance and accessibility. You will work closely with frontend development team to deliver exceptional user experiences. This role requires a strong commitment to user experience and performance.

Key Responsibilities:
- Develop and maintain responsive and interactive user interfaces using React, Angular, or Vue.js.
- Translate UI/UX designs and wireframes into high-quality code.
- Optimize web applications for maximum speed, scalability, and cross-browser compatibility.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Web Development, or a related field or equivalent practical experience.
- 2+ years of proven professional experience in Frontend Developer or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in JavaScript (ES6+), HTML5, CSS3, React/Angular/Vue.js.
- Demonstrated experience with Responsive Design, RESTful APIs, Git, Webpack/Vite.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with TypeScript, Redux/Vuex/NgRx, Jest/React Testing Library, Tailwind CSS, Figma.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- creating visually appealing and highly interactive web applications.
