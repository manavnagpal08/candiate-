Job Title: Customer Support Specialist

About Us:
We are a leading company in customer service and technical support looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to deliver exceptional customer experiences and build lasting relationships.

The Role:
As a Customer Support Specialist, you will be responsible for resolving customer inquiries efficiently and empathetically, providing comprehensive product assistance and troubleshooting, and maintaining high levels of customer satisfaction and loyalty. You will work closely with customer support team to ensure positive customer interactions and product adoption. This role requires a strong commitment to customer empathy and problem-solving.

Key Responsibilities:
- Respond to customer inquiries via phone, email, chat, and other channels in a timely and professional manner.
- Diagnose and resolve product or service issues, guiding customers through solutions step-by-step.
- Document all customer interactions, issues, and resolutions accurately in the CRM system.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Customer Service, Communications, Information Technology, or a related field or equivalent practical experience.
- 1+ year of proven professional experience in Customer Support Specialist or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Customer Service, Problem Solving, Communication (written & verbal), Active Listening.
- Demonstrated experience with CRM Software (e.g., Zendesk, Salesforce Service Cloud), Troubleshooting, Product Knowledge.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Ticketing Systems, Technical Aptitude, Empathy, Multitasking, Call Center experience.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- being the direct voice of our company and making a difference for our users.
