Job Title: Civil Structural Engineer

About Us:
We are a leading company in civil engineering and construction looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to design safe, sustainable, and innovative infrastructure projects.

The Role:
As a Civil Structural Engineer, you will be responsible for designing structural elements for buildings and bridges, performing structural analysis and calculations, and preparing engineering drawings and specifications. You will work closely with civil and structural engineering team to ensure the structural integrity and safety of construction projects. This role requires a strong commitment to safety and structural integrity.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design structural components (concrete, steel, timber) for various civil engineering projects.
- Perform complex structural analysis using industry-standard software (e.g., SAP2000, ETABS, STAAD.Pro).
- Prepare detailed engineering drawings, specifications, and reports in compliance with codes.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Civil Engineering, Structural Engineering, or a related field or equivalent practical experience.
- 3+ years of proven professional experience in Civil Structural Engineer or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Structural Analysis, Concrete Design, Steel Design, AutoCAD.
- Demonstrated experience with SAP2000, ETABS, STAAD.Pro, Building Codes (e.g., IBC, ASCE).
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with BIM Software (Revit), Geotechnical Engineering, Project Management.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- designing foundational infrastructure that shapes communities.
