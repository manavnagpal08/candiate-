Job Title: Technical Project Manager

About Us:
We are a leading company in software development and technology delivery looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to deliver complex technical projects on time and within budget.

The Role:
As a Technical Project Manager, you will be responsible for leading cross-functional technical project teams, defining project scope, goals, and deliverables, and managing project risks, schedules, and budgets. You will work closely with engineering and product teams to ensure successful execution and delivery of technical initiatives. This role requires a strong commitment to execution excellence and technical understanding.

Key Responsibilities:
- Develop comprehensive project plans, including scope, timelines, resource allocation, and budget.
- Facilitate agile ceremonies (e.g., sprint planning, stand-ups, retrospectives) and remove impediments.
- Communicate project status, risks, and dependencies effectively to all stakeholders.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Project Management or equivalent practical experience.
- 5+ years of proven professional experience in Technical Project Manager or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Technical Project Management, Agile Methodologies (Scrum/Kanban), SDLC.
- Demonstrated experience with Jira, Confluence, Risk Management, Stakeholder Management.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with PMP/CSM certification, Cloud Technologies, Software Architecture understanding.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- driving the successful delivery of our core technology initiatives.
