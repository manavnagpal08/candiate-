Job Title: Technical Writer

About Us:
We are a leading company in technical documentation and content creation looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to provide clear, concise, and accurate technical information to our users and developers.

The Role:
As a Technical Writer, you will be responsible for creating comprehensive user manuals and guides, developing clear API documentation and release notes, and maintaining and improving our knowledge base and help content. You will work closely with product, engineering, and support teams to enhance user understanding and product usability through effective documentation. This role requires a strong commitment to clarity and accuracy.

Key Responsibilities:
- Write, edit, and maintain high-quality technical documentation for software products and APIs.
- Collaborate with subject matter experts (SMEs) to gather information and ensure technical accuracy.
- Structure and organize content for optimal readability, searchability, and user experience.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Technical Communication, English, Computer Science, or a related field or equivalent practical experience.
- 2+ years of proven professional experience in Technical Writer or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Technical Writing, Content Management Systems (CMS), Documentation Tools.
- Demonstrated experience with Markdown, XML/HTML, API Documentation, Microsoft Office Suite.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with MadCap Flare, Confluence, Git, Adobe FrameMaker, DITA, UX writing principles.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- making complex technology understandable and accessible to a wide audience.
