Job Title: Event Manager

About Us:
We are a leading company in event planning and management looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to create memorable and impactful events that achieve business objectives.

The Role:
As a Event Manager, you will be responsible for planning and executing corporate events and conferences, managing event budgets and vendor relationships, and overseeing event logistics and on-site operations. You will work closely with marketing and events team to deliver successful events that enhance brand reputation and engagement. This role requires a strong commitment to meticulous planning and seamless execution.

Key Responsibilities:
- Plan and execute a variety of corporate events, conferences, webinars, and trade shows from conception to completion.
- Manage event budgets, negotiate contracts with vendors, and ensure cost-effectiveness.
- Oversee all event logistics, including venue selection, catering, AV, registration, and on-site management.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Event Management, Marketing, Hospitality, or Business Administration or equivalent practical experience.
- 4+ years of proven professional experience in Event Manager or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Event Planning, Budget Management, Vendor Management.
- Demonstrated experience with Logistics Coordination, Project Management, Communication Skills.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Event Management Software (e.g., Cvent, Eventbrite), Marketing Automation, Public Relations, Sponsorship Management.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- creating impactful experiences that connect our brand with its audience.
